RDA value vocabularies

RDA Conventional Collective Title

A title used as the preferred title for a compilation containing two or more works by one person, family, or corporate body, or two or more parts of a work (e.g., Works, Poems, Selections).

Number of concepts: 10
Namespace: http://rdaregistry.info/termList/CollTitle
Suggested prefix*: rdacct
Example curie: rdacct:1001
Changelog feed: Atom | RDF

* registered at prefix.cc
